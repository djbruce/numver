As noted elsewhere our reliance on numerical linear algebra means our computations are never 100% precise, and there is always room for error. In particular, we cannot be sure that the Betti numbers we compute are the true Betti numbers and not simply an approximation. Thus, whenever possible we work to verify our computations in various ways. Throughout the codes Rk-V, Gb-V, MgGb-V, etc. refer to the following verification methods.

Rk-V. Rank Verification. This means we have checked our rank computations using MatLab's standard dense rank algorithm rank(A), and the results agree up to machine precision with our methods.
